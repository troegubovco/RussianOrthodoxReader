Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 6, 2026

Russian Orthodox Reader ("the App") is designed to help users read Scripture and follow the liturgical calendar of the Russian Orthodox Church. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

Network Requests

The App connects to orthocal.info to retrieve daily liturgical readings, saints, fasting information, and tone of the week. These requests contain only the calendar date and no personally identifiable information.

Local Storage

All app data — including cached readings, the biblical dictionary, and user preferences (such as font size) — is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
