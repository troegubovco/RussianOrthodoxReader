About the App

Russian Orthodox Reader provides the full Russian Synodal Bible, a biblical dictionary with over 6,800 entries, daily liturgical readings from the Orthodox calendar, and a Paschal calendar — all in one app.

Features

  • Full Russian Synodal Bible text
  • Biblical dictionary with 6,800+ definitions and conjugation lookup
  • Daily liturgical readings, saints, and fasting information
  • Paschal calendar and liturgical calendar
  • Adjustable font size for comfortable reading

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the daily readings come from?
Liturgical data is provided by orthocal.info and is cached on your device for offline access.

Does the app work offline?
The Bible text and dictionary are fully available offline. Liturgical readings are cached after the first load.

How do I change the font size?
Go to the Settings tab and adjust the font size slider.
